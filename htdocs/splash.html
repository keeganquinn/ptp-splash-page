Personal Telco is an All Volunteer Non Profit Running a City Wide Network

We are dedicated to the idea that users have acentral role in how their communications networks are operated.

We educate folks on how they can operate a node andparticipate in a free wifi network for the common good. Our goal isto have people and businesses in every corner of the city andregion offering connectivity to each other.

If you're a business owner it's likely you're already payingfor internet service. Most businesses use a miniscual amount of thebandwidth they have bought. We can help you offer that extrabandwidth to your customers while having no impact on your existingnetwork and operations. We just need to connect to one ethernetport on an existing router.

As a homeowner or renter you may be interested inparticipating by either sharing your residential network connectionwith your neighbors or connecting to an existing PTP Node as arepeater. Your participation in PTP's network helps us to smotherthe city in free wifi. There is plenty of bandwidth to go around.

We offer node hosts support and equipment and advize them onsuch things as network providers and networking equipment and tryto be responsive to outages.

Join the cause and help us extend the PTP Network throughoutthe Portland metropolitan area!
